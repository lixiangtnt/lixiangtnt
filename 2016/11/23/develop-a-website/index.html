1.Introduction

Nowadays, the services are providing gradually simple way to develop a website compared with old age. For now, you do not need to have over years website developing skills to do the website coding job. Everything is perfect wrapping up for you to utilize. What you need to do is only to follow the instruction step by step. You can teach yourself as web developer. In this very practical case, I don’t think university eduction is essential for many type of jobs in the future.
This article summarizes the step by step how to develop your own website via your custom domain.
